Yam Chips

Description

The yam chips is thesame thing as oyinbo chips that is made with potato the only difference is that instead of potato we use yam here. It should be easy to make and should take about 30 mins to make

Ingeidients

  • Yam
  • Seasoning
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Peel and cut yam to chips form
  2. Cook yam for about 10 minutes
  3. Sieve yam out
  4. Spray yam with oil and seasoning
  5. Fry Yam
  6. Eat yam snd enjoy

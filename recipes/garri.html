Groceries(Garri)

Description

Groceries is a staple food in every Nigerian home. It is most popular to students because of its easy preparation time. In less than 10 minutes it should be ready for consumption. Sha make sure that you hide your stash before friends and family search your fridge an chop your garri finish.

Ingeidients

  • Garri
  • Sugar
  • Groundnut (optional)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of garri to bowl
  2. Add 3 spoons of sugar to same bowl
  3. Add groundnut if availabe
  4. Soak in water and mix
  5. Eat and enjoy Groceries

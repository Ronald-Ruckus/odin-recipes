Beans

Description

I actually don't like beans but comparedto other Nigerian dishes I prefer it to be on my top 3. It takes time to make and e dey cause person to mess. Na these two reasons why me no like am. But e no cost to make and it gives energy.

Ingeidients

  • Beans
  • Salt
  • Seasoning
  • Palm oil

Steps

  1. Pick beans so there would not be dirt in ya food
  2. Cook beans in appropriate amount of water for about 45 mins or until soft
  3. Add more water
  4. Add palm oil and seasoning
  5. Mix well andlet cook for about 30 mins
  6. Goodluck and enjoy your beans
  7. Leave where people are if you want to mess after eating the beans
